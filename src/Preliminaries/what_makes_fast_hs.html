The Four Horsemen of Bad Haskell Performance¶

There are four horsemen of the poorly performing Haskell code. In this section, we describe each of the four horsemen in isolation so that an interested reader may triage their own issues. A reader should come away from this section with an understanding of the central ways a Haskell program slows down.
